How to use the Yield Sheet:

For each full screen you make, enter the the number of full screens of donuts.

If you have a last partial screen, enter a plus (+) and the number of individual donuts on the screen.

For example: if you make 10 full screens of glazed donuts and you have 16 extra glazed donuts on your last screen, you would enter 10+16 into the input field.
