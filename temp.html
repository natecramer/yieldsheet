Temperature in degrees Fahrenheit (°F)




Steps:
1. Use your thermometer to check the room temperature, input it into the Room Temperature field.

2. Stick the thermometer probe into the dough mix bag to check the temperature, input it into the Mix Temperature field.

3. Adjust the sink water to what the resulting Water Temperature field reads and add exactly 3 gallons of water to the mixing bowl.

4. Add 1 full bag of dough mix to the bowl.

5. Weigh and add exactly 11 ounces of dry yeast.

6:
- If using a Globe Mixer:
- Mix for 1 minute on Gear 1, then
- Mix for 10 minutes on Gear 2

- If using a Hobart Mixer:
- Mix for 1 minute on 2nd gear, then
- Mix for 10 minutes on 4th gear (cheese shred setting)

7. After mixing, check the final dough temperature. It must be between 77°F and 80°F.
